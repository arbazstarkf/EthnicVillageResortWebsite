Ethnic Village: Where Nature and Culture Unite

Discover the beauty Northeast India at Ethnic Village Resort, where nature's wonders and cultural traditions come to life. Nestled in the lush hills, our resort offers a harmonious blend of luxury and nature. Delight your taste buds with a fusion of ethnic and continental cuisine. Gather around a bonfire for a night of family, friends, fireworks, and local folk dance. Immerse in local traditions with traditional dance shows. Stay in clean, cozy accommodations surrounded by nature. Engage in over 10 outdoor activities for all ages, enjoy bait fishing, and fly kites. Host events from picnics to parties, and take the stage for performances. Local singer programs and sound systems are also available to enhance your experience. At Ethnic Village Resort, embrace the magic of nature and create cherished memories in a warm, cultural atmosphere. Your extraordinary journey begins here.
