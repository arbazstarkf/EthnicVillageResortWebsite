What We Offer

This place is the ultimate ethnic escape, ideal for turning any ordinary Sunday or holiday into an extraordinary adventure with your family or friends. Immerse yourself in the pure, refreshing natural atmosphere that engulfs you, providing an expansive playground for kids and sports enthusiasts to indulge in spirited football or cricket matches. As for the food, it's a culinary paradise. We not only serve light, home-cooked dishes, but we also offer a tantalizing array of Continental delicacies, fast food favorites, and traditional Assamese masterpieces, including the incredibly mouthwatering Masor Tenga. And here's the showstopper: when you order fish, we fetch it straight from our ponds and transform it into a culinary masterpiece, just for you. It's an experience that goes above and beyond, making your visit truly unforgettable!
